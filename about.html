About Government Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital (GPN) is one of the premier technical institutes in Uttarakhand, functioning under the aegis of the Department of Technical Education, Government of Uttarakhand. The institute is affiliated to the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education Roorkee (UBTER) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Located amidst the serene hills of Nainital, the institute offers diploma-level education in engineering and technology. GPN is committed to providing industry-relevant technical education that prepares students to meet the challenges of modern industries and emerging technologies.

GPN Nainital emphasizes practical knowledge and hands-on training through well-equipped labs, experienced faculty, and regular industry exposure. The institute also supports extracurricular development through NCC, sports, cultural activities, and various student clubs.

Over the years, Government Polytechnic Nainital has played a significant role in producing skilled diploma engineers who have excelled in various sectors including government services, private industries, and entrepreneurship.
